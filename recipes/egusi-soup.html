Egusi Soup

Description

This is a very enjoyable meal, best served with pounded yam. It is made of thickened grounded melon seeds and contains fish, and/or meat as well as leafy vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Grounded egusi
  • Palm oil
  • Sliced onions
  • Grounded pepper
  • Blended crayfish
  • Stock fish
  • Meat, Fish or Chicken
  • Leafy vegetable
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Turn the egusi into a paste by mixing with some cups of water or stock from the meat, fish or chicken
  2. Put a pot on a stove and pour some palm oil, set to low heat
  3. Add the stock and set your cooker to low heat to simmer
  4. Add salt and grounded crayfish
  5. Add the egusi paste and stir
  6. Leave to simmer for 5 minutes
  7. Add your meat,fish or chicken and the other ingredients you have prepared
  8. Add the vegetables
  9. Stir and allow to simmer for about 5 mins
  10. Stir and taste to check if the seasoning is enough, add more if need be
  11. Leave for 2 minutes and your soup is ready
